About Us

Growing Farmers’ Future

Brundavan Nursery, operating under Brundavan Agro, is committed to supporting farmers with high-quality vegetable saplings. Located in Sangareddy district, we use proven nursery practices to ensure healthy plant growth, uniformity, and better yield outcomes.

We aim to become a trusted partner for farmers by delivering consistent quality and dependable supply across Telangana.
